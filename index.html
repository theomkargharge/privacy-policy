3. How the App Works

3.1 Storage Access Framework (SAF)

Our app uses Android's official Storage Access Framework (SAF) to access WhatsApp status files:

  • You manually grant access to the WhatsApp folder using Android's system file picker
  • This permission is stored locally on your device only
  • We cannot access any other folders or files on your device
  • This access can be revoked at any time through your device settings
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any servers)

3.2 Local Storage Only

All operations happen entirely on your device:

  • Downloaded statuses are saved locally in your device's gallery
  • Files are stored in: Pictures/StatusSaver (for images) and Movies/StatusSaver (for videos)
  • Saved favorites are stored in your device's internal storage
  • No files are uploaded to any server or cloud service
  • All data remains under your control at all times

3.3 Offline Operation

The app works completely offline:

  • No internet connection required for core functionality
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • Your files and privacy remain on your device
  • Internet is only used for displaying advertisements (if applicable)

4. Permissions Required

The app requires the following Android permissions. Here's why we need each one:

4.1 Storage Permissions (Android 13+)

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To read WhatsApp image statuses from your device
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO: To read WhatsApp video statuses from your device
  • Purpose: Required to view and save WhatsApp statuses
  • Scope: Limited to the specific folder you grant access to via SAF

4.2 Storage Permissions (Android 11-12)

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To access the WhatsApp status folder
  • Purpose: Required to view and save WhatsApp statuses
  • Scope: Limited to the folder you explicitly select

4.3 Internet Permission (If Using Ads)

  • INTERNET: Only for displaying advertisements (if applicable)
  • Purpose: To load and display ads from Google AdMob
  • Note: No user data is transmitted; only ad content is loaded
